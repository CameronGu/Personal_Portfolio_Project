Approach

Building and Problem Solving

I’ve spent over a decade living in Costa Rica while running U.S.-based businesses that serve clients worldwide. As the founder of Already There Travel, I lead a team that simplifies travel logistics for some of the world’s most ambitious projects, helping companies save time, reduce costs, and achieve their goals.

My work begins with the belief that every problem has a solution. With curiosity, persistence, and the right tools, I build systems that turn complexity into clarity. Whether coding tools to streamline operations, consulting for small businesses, or navigating challenges in unexpected ways, I focus on creating value through thoughtful solutions.

Simplicity and efficiency have become guiding principles for me. They create the space to focus on what matters: solving problems well, empowering others, and enjoying the process.

Work

What I Do

    Founder at Already There Travel: I designed a travel management system that supports Managed Services Providers in scaling large, global multi-million-dollar projects that span continents. Leading an exceptional team that operates across languages, cultures, and time zones, we solve logistical problems before they arise—saving clients time, money, and stress while becoming an essential part of their operations.
    Social Impact Investor: Through PrestaMio and Nativ Capital, I provide tailored financing and strategic business advice to entrepreneurs in underserved communities in Costa Rica. I believe that access to capital and knowledge is a catalyst for change, and I’m proud of the jobs, opportunities, and economic growth these investments have created.
    Community Servant and Leader: To unite my community in Costa Rica, I founded the organizations Visit Cahuita and The Cahuita Community Group Alliance. Through these efforts, I’ve connected people, supported local businesses, and strengthened the tourism economy in Costa Rica’s Southern Caribbean region—my adopted home. Beyond economic initiatives, we’ve raised critical funds for urgent medical care, supported food kitchens, and provided direct aid to countless families during the pandemic. By bringing together local and foreign communities, these initiatives foster collaboration, advocacy, and meaningful change.
